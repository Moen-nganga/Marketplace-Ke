How It Works

SellAnythingKE is Kenya's free peer-to-peer marketplace. Here's everything you need to know.

What is a P2P Marketplace?

P2P stands for peer-to-peer. This means that on SellAnythingKE, individuals buy and sell directly with each other — there is no middleman, no warehouse, and no company holding your goods. You deal directly with the person selling or buying the item.

This makes buying and selling faster, cheaper, and more personal. The seller sets the price, the buyer negotiates directly, and both parties agree on how and where to complete the transaction.

How to Buy

1

Browse or Search

Use the search bar or browse categories to find what you are looking for. You can filter results by price, condition, and location to narrow down your options.

2

View the Listing

Click on any listing to see full details — photos, description, condition, reason for selling, and the seller's profile with their ratings and reviews from other buyers.

3

Contact the Seller

Use the in-app encrypted messaging system to chat with the seller, ask questions, and negotiate the price. You can also call or WhatsApp them directly if they have shared their phone number.

4

Meet and Inspect

Agree on a safe public meeting point. Inspect the item carefully before making any payment. If everything checks out, complete the transaction on the spot.

5

Rate the Seller

After completing your purchase, visit the seller's profile and leave a rating and review. This helps other buyers make informed decisions and rewards honest sellers.

How to Sell

1

Create a Free Account

Register on SellAnythingKE with your name, email, and phone number. Your account is free and takes less than a minute to set up.

2

Post Your Ad

Click the "+ Post Ad" button and fill in your listing details — title, category, condition, price, location, description, and up to 6 photos. Add tags to help buyers find your item more easily.

3

Wait for Buyers

Your listing goes live instantly and is visible to all users browsing or searching in your category. Interested buyers will message you or call you directly.

4

Negotiate and Agree

Chat with interested buyers through the platform's encrypted messaging system. Discuss the price, answer their questions, and agree on a meeting point that is convenient and safe for both of you.

5

Complete the Sale

Meet the buyer, hand over the item, collect your payment, and mark the listing as sold on your profile. It's that simple.
