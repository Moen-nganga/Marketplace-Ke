Safety Tips

Stay safe while buying and selling on SellAnythingKE

⚠️ Important Warning

SellAnythingKE will never ask you to send money via M-Pesa, bank transfer, or any payment method before meeting in person. If someone claims to be from SellAnythingKE and asks for payment, it is a scam. Report them immediately.

When Buying

🤝

Always Meet in Person

Arrange to meet the seller face to face before making any payment. Choose a public, busy location such as a shopping mall, petrol station, or bank. Never agree to pay for items till they have been delivered and you have confirmed that the item is genuine.

🔍

Inspect Before You Pay

Always inspect the item thoroughly before handing over any money. Test electronics, check for damage, verify serial numbers, and confirm the item matches the listing description. If the seller refuses to let you inspect the item, walk away.

💰

Be Cautious of Deals That Seem Too Good

If a price seems unusually low for the item being sold, be very careful. Scammers often list high-value items at extremely low prices to attract victims. Always research the market price before agreeing to buy.

📞

Verify the Seller's Identity

Check the seller's profile rating and reviews from other buyers. A seller with no reviews and a brand new account selling expensive items should raise a red flag. Use our in-app chat to communicate and keep a record of all conversations.

🚫

Never Pay in Advance

Do not send any payment — including deposits — before receiving and inspecting the item. Scammers often ask for a deposit to "reserve" an item and then disappear. Legitimate sellers will not require advance payment.

🏦

Use Safe Payment Methods

Prefer paying in cash at the point of exchange. If you must use M-Pesa, only send money after you have received and verified the item. Never pay via Western Union, gift cards, or cryptocurrency as these are impossible to recover if you are scammed.

When Selling

✅

Confirm Payment Before Releasing Items

Always confirm that M-Pesa or cash has been received before handing over your item. With M-Pesa, check your messages directly — do not trust screenshots sent by the buyer as these can be faked.

📸

Use Real Photos of Your Item

Always use actual photos of the item you are selling, not photos from the internet. This builds trust with buyers and protects you from disputes about the item's condition.

🕵️

Watch Out for Fake Buyers

Be cautious of buyers who offer to pay more than your asking price, ask you to ship items before payment, or pressure you to complete the transaction quickly. These are common scam tactics.

📍

Choose Safe Meeting Spots

When meeting a buyer, choose a well-lit, public location. Let someone you trust know where you are going and who you are meeting. For high-value transactions, consider meeting at a police station or bank.

🚨

Report Suspicious Activity

If you encounter a suspicious listing or user, report them immediately using the report button on their profile or listing. Help us keep SellAnythingKE safe for everyone.
