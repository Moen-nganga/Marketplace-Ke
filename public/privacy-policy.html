Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Marketplace Ke collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our platform. By using Marketplace Ke, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

When you register and use Marketplace Ke, we collect the following information:

  • Account Information — your full name, email address, and phone number when you register.
  • Profile Information — your profile photo if you choose to upload one.
  • Listing Information — details of items you post for sale, including photos, descriptions, prices, and location.
  • Messages — communications between buyers and sellers through our encrypted messaging system. Messages are encrypted end-to-end and we cannot read their contents.
  • Usage Data — information about how you use the platform, such as pages visited and searches made.
  • Ratings and Reviews — feedback you give or receive from other users.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage your account on Marketplace Ke.
  • To allow you to post listings and communicate with other users.
  • To display your public profile, listings, and ratings to other users.
  • To send you important notifications about your account or listings.
  • To improve the platform and fix technical issues.
  • To detect and prevent fraud, scams, and abuse of the platform.
  • To comply with legal obligations under Kenyan law.

3. Information We Share

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We only share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With Other Users — your name, profile photo, listings, and ratings are visible to other users on the platform. Your phone number is only shared if you choose to include it in your listing.
  • With Service Providers — we may share information with trusted third-party services that help us operate the platform, such as hosting providers. These providers are bound by confidentiality agreements.
  • For Legal Reasons — we may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government authority in Kenya.
  • To Prevent Harm — we may share information if we believe it is necessary to prevent fraud, illegal activity, or harm to any person.

4. Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously and implement appropriate technical measures to protect it:

  • All passwords are securely hashed and never stored in plain text.
  • All messages between users are end-to-end encrypted using AES-256-GCM encryption. We cannot read the content of your messages.
  • User sessions are protected using JSON Web Tokens (JWT).
  • We use secure HTTPS connections to protect data in transit.

However, no system is completely secure. We encourage you to use a strong password and to report any suspicious activity on your account immediately.

5. Your Rights

As a user of Marketplace Ke, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — you can view your account information at any time through your profile page.
  • Correction — you can update your name, phone number, and profile photo through your profile settings.
  • Deletion — you can delete your listings at any time. To request full account deletion, contact us and we will remove your data within 30 days.
  • Portability — you may request a copy of your personal data that we hold.

6. Cookies and Tracking

Marketplace Ke uses browser local storage to keep you logged in and remember your session. We do not use advertising cookies or tracking pixels. We do not share your browsing data with advertisers.

7. Children's Privacy

Marketplace Ke is not intended for use by persons under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has created an account on our platform, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

8. Third-Party Links

Our platform may contain links to third-party websites or services, such as WhatsApp. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties and encourage you to review their privacy policies before sharing any personal information with them.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make significant changes, we will notify users through the platform. Your continued use of Marketplace Ke after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us through the Contact Us page. We are committed to resolving any concerns you may have.
